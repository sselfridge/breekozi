Additional Services

Regular World History & U.S. History Help

Although Bree does not offer a comprehensive weekly program for non-AP History class, she does offer Regular World History and Regular U.S. History private tutoring on an as-needed basis, as well as group Finals reviews in the fall and spring.

English Book Review Crash Courses

Bree offers a variety of one-hour book reviews, which serve as a crash course for assigned reading books. By reviewing the context, plot, characters, themes, quotes, vocabulary, etc., Bree helps students deepen their understanding of the assigned novel or play in preparation for their upcoming assessments.

Sample Book Reviews:

  • Fahrenheit 451, The Princess Bride, Night
  • To Kill a Mockingbird, Of Mice and Men, Romeo and Juliet, Anthem
  • The Picture of Dorian Gray, Lord of the Flies, 12 Angry Men, Much Ado About Nothing, Brave New World, 1984, A Separate Peace, Macbeth
  • The Crucible, The Adventures of Huckleberry Finn, The Great Gatsby
  • Hamlet, Frankenstein
  • Additional Book Reviews may be added based on demand.

Math with Michele Koziara

With nearly twenty years of experience, Michele Koziara is the Math expert. She is well-versed in both Traditional Math (Pre-Algebra, Geometry, Algebra-1, Algebra-2) and Common Core Math (Math-7, Math-8, Math-1 & Math-1 Enhanced, Math-2 & Math 2-Enhanced, Math-3). While many Math teachers know the subject matter, they can’t articulate their thoughts and students are left struggling and frustrated. Mrs. K breaks down difficult concepts and helps her students develop the foundational knowledge and skills they need to succeed.

Beyond her expert instruction and warm personality, Michele also provides her students with valuable resources with which to practice the concepts they’ll be tested on, helping to eliminate test-day stress and anxiety.

Please email Michele at mkoziara1@gmail.com with all math inquiries.
