Bree Kozi, Ed.M. Harvard University

Bree Kozi, Ed.M. Harvard University, is an experienced teacher who brings a unique and innovative approach to education and student development. Bree graduated top of her class from USC and earned her Master’s in Higher Education from Harvard. She began tutoring and mentoring in 2002 and has been doing so on a full-time basis for nearly a decade as the founder of academic stratEDGEy.

While many teachers go through the motions or strictly teach to the test, Bree’s passion for learning and teaching shine through in everything she does. Beyond simply preparing students for upcoming assessments, Bree serves as an academic mentor by helping students successfully navigate their academic career, develop their critical thinking ability, and build skills for success.
